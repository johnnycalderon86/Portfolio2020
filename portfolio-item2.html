BMI-calculator

This was my first dive into the Flutter experience. Coming from building an app with React Native the feeling I was left with after working with Flutter, was its intuitive way of building the UI. It is sort of like building with Legos. You extend a widget which could have any amount of properties which you then manipulate to your liking. It was also nice discovering Dart and strong typed languages.
