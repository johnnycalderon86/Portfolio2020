First project Lowie

CHO portion counter

The opportunity to be part of this great application was given to me by the founder of Nebulae. The application was made to ease the life of a client by tracking carbohydrate portions for every item in a meal. The application would sum all the single items and make a meal with a total sum of carbohydrates.

If you would like to try out this application press the link Lowie and download the Expo app to scan the QR-code
